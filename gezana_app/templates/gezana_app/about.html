About Gezana

Welcome to Gezana, a warm and authentic Habesha restaurant located in the heart of Dublin.

Our menu celebrates traditional Ethiopian and Eritrean cuisine, featuring classic dishes such as injera, doro wat, tibs, shiro, and more — all prepared with love and heritage.

At Gezana, we believe food is a way of bringing people together. We create an atmosphere where guests can enjoy delicious meals, cultural hospitality, and unforgettable experiences.

Whether you’re new to Habesha cuisine or returning for familiar flavors, Gezana is delighted to welcome you.
