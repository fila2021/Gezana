Our Story

Gezana brings Habesha warmth and flavor to Dublin

We serve the dishes we grew up with: feasts built on injera, slow-simmered stews, and coffee poured in three rounds for blessings and conversation.

Signature Ritual

Traditional Coffee Ceremony

Roast • Grind • Brew in a jebena • Share three rounds: abol, tona, baraka.

Our Cuisine

Soft, tangy injera is the heart of the table, with doro wat, tibs, shiro, and fresh veggie sides served family-style. Berbere and mitmita spices add depth, heat, and warmth to every bite.

Coffee Ceremony

We hand-roast beans until glossy, grind them fresh, and brew in a clay jebena. The aroma fills the room as we pour three rounds—each shared for friendship and good fortune.

Hospitality

We welcome you like family: generous portions, injera for scooping, and stories of home. Whether it’s your first time or you’re craving familiar flavors, we’re here to host you.

Our Promise

Fresh ingredients, traditional methods, and a table set for sharing. Bring friends, family, or colleagues—Gezana is your home for Habesha food, coffee, and culture.
