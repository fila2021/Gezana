Our Cuisine

Soft, tangy injera is the heart of the table, with doro wat, tibs, shiro, and veggie sides served family-style. Berbere and mitmita add warmth and depth.

Coffee Ceremony

We hand-roast beans until glossy, grind them fresh, and brew in a clay jebena. The aroma fills the room as we pour three rounds for friendship and good fortune.

Hospitality

We welcome you like family: generous portions, injera for scooping, and stories of home. Whether it’s your first time or a familiar craving—welcome.

Our Promise

Fresh ingredients, traditional methods, and a table set for sharing. Bring friends, family, or colleagues—Gezana is your home for Habesha food and culture.
